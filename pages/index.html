Why should you choose Aurora Home
Health Aide Agency?

Aurora Home Care offers a full suite of home health nursing services, companion care, and rehabilitative therapy tailored to the needs of senior citizens, individuals with disabilities, and patients in rehabilitation. We collaborate with you to design a personalized care plan to meet your unique needs.

It's crucial to understand that our caregivers, while all highly trained to deliver superior care, come from different backgrounds and possess varying skills.

Skilled caregivers are those with training in the medical field, such as nurses and therapists, capable of providing specialized healthcare services. On the other hand, non-skilled caregivers are there to assist with personal care, companionship, transportation, housekeeping, and various other daily tasks you may require assistance with within your home.
